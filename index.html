Rome is the historic capital of Italy. It is littered with ancient ruins such as the Forum and the Colosseum, which showcase the glory and power of the Roman Empire. It is also known for having a Country within the city. Vatican City is the headquarters of the Roman Catholic Church and also the Vatican Museums, which houses masterpieces that date back to many centuries ago.

Rome was called the “Eternal City” by the ancient Romans, as they believed that the city’s legacy will remain forever. Because of its one of a kind history, the city is the third most visited city in Europe and fourteenth in the world. It attracts almost 10 million tourists from across the world, who have discovering Rome’s impressive monuments on the top of their to-do list. The city is also well-known for its impeccable cuisine and lovable people.
