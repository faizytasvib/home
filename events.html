There are many amazing celebrations in rome, which take place all throughout the year, in which hundreds of people come out on the streets and celebrate.

Date Name Description Picture
17 March Unification of Italy A National feast day, it is celebrated on the anniversary of the forming of the Kingdom of Italy.
25 April Day of National Liberation This date remembers the general uprising that freed most of northern Italy from the Nazi occupation.
Somewhere from 5 February-March 9 Carnevale di Roma An eight-day carnival with colourful floats, magicians, jugglers, dancers, musicians.
April Rome Marathon An annual marathon competition held by the city of Rome.
November 8 Festa dei Colori a Roma The Festival of Colours. Honours the hindu festival, where thousands of people throw coloured powders.
September Taste of Roma Rome’s largest food festival, a four-day food celebration is an event in which you can taste various foods.
March 15 Ides of March Rome marks the anniversary of Julius Caesar’s death with a reenactment and other celebrations to honour him.
December Christmas in Rome Christmas is one of the most wonderful times of the year in Rome, the city comes alive with religious celebrations.
